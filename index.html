Our History

General Fashion Academy is a small-scale dressmaking firm that has been in business since 1994. It is one of the 15 companies selected in 2002 by the government of Ghana to launch the President’s Special Initiative (PSI) on textile and garment to take advantage of AGOA (Africa Growth & Opportunity Act) & also one of the most recognized dressmaking firms in the Kumasi Metropolis.

Admission Process

We appreciate your interest in our institution. We are excited to introduce you to the general fashion academy community, and our website is a terrific location to learn more about everything we have to offer.

How to get the forms:

  1. Purchase application form at the front desk of the school.
  2. Pay through Via Momo 024 426 4872 (General Fashion Academy). Send a screenshot to 024 426 4872 of Momo transaction.

Cost & Payments

Important Notice

Application forms should be purchased only at schools premises (USE THE SERIAL NUMBER AND PIN TO FILL THE FORM ON generalfashionacademyonlineforms.online)

  • Tuition fee shall be paid every year.
  • Fees paid are not refundable.
  • Tuition, miscellaneous & maintenance fees must be paid at the office or through momo or at the bank.
  • Miscellaneous covers school uniform and student ID card.
  • You can pay 70% of the tuition (or full amount).
